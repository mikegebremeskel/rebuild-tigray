## What they do

Omna Tigray is a diaspora-led advocacy organization focused on accountability for atrocities committed during the 2020–2022 genocide in Tigray. The organization works across three areas: documenting human rights violations, engaging policymakers in the United States and internationally, and building public awareness about the situation in Tigray.

Since its founding in 2020, Omna Tigray has briefed members of Congress, submitted testimony to international bodies, and produced detailed reports on the humanitarian and human rights situation on the ground.

## Their approach

The work is systematic and evidence-based. Omna Tigray collects and organizes documentation of abuses, then channels that evidence into policy advocacy. The goal is not just attention but accountability — pushing for investigations, sanctions, and formal recognition of what happened in Tigray.

The organization also runs a public-facing communications function, translating complex policy and legal developments into accessible language for the diaspora and the general public.

## Key focus areas

- Congressional and executive branch engagement in the United States
- International advocacy at the United Nations and African Union
- Documentation of human rights violations
- Public communications and diaspora outreach
